Frequently Asked Questions

What is Close, and who is it for?

Close is an all-in-one CRM platform built specifically for small and scaling sales teams. It combines contact management, built-in communication (calling, emailing, SMS), automation, and reportingso you can manage leads, work deals, and close faster without relying on a messy tech stack.

Do I need to integrate other tools to communicate with leads?

Nope. Close has native calling, emailing, SMS, and calendar tools built right in. That means your team can reach out, follow up, and schedule meetings directly from the CRMwithout third-party dialers, plugins, or email tools.

How does Close help my team follow up with leads more effectively

Your team can build automated sales sequences that include email, SMS, and task remindersso no lead ever slips through the cracks. You also get productivity tools like Smart Views, templates, and Power Dialer to streamline outreach at scale.

Can Close help me track performance and improve my sales process?

Yes. Close includes real-time dashboards, pipeline tracking, and activity-based reporting so you can monitor individual and team performance. With AI-powered coaching and deal insights (coming soon), youll also gain actionable guidance on how to improve win rates and deal velocity.

Is Close easy to set up and use?

Very. Close is designed to be fast, intuitive, and low-maintenanceso your team can get started without needing developers or consultants. You can customize pipelines, fields, and workflows in just a few clicks, and our support team is always here to help if you need it.
