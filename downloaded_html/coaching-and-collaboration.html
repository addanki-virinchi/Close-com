Frequently Asked Questions

Can I listen to my reps calls live in Close?

Yes! With Live Call Listening, Whisper and Barge modes, you can join active calls to observe or coach in real timewithout interrupting the conversation.

How can I review and give feedback on rep calls?

Every call is automatically recorded and transcribed, making it easy to review key moments, leave feedback, and even share standout examples with the team.

How can I review and give feedback on rep calls?

Use built-in Activity and Funnel reports to monitor call volume, email activity, meeting booking rates, and lead conversionso you can spot trends and coach more effectively.

Can I help new reps ramp faster with Close?

Absolutely. Smart Views, shared templates, and automated workflows provide structure and guidance, while recordings and call examples help them learn by doing.

Are Closes coaching features like call recordings and live listening included in my plan?

Features like call recordings, live listening, and whisper coaching are included on the Enterprise plantheres no separate add-on or coaching-specific fee. Just choose the plan that fits your teams needs.
