Pipedrive products and features FAQs

  • CRM products refer to the sales software platform, features belonging to higher tier plans, any other add-ons that give the CRM additional features and Marketplace integrations that sync your CRM data with third-party products.

  • A good CRM solution allows large and small businesses to easily track their deals, offer a foundation for marketing activities and generate leads for the sales pipeline. It also simplifies document and contract creation, with the flexibility to customize the CRM around the organization’s unique sales processes.

  • Without a good selection of helpful products, a CRM is little better than a spreadsheet.
